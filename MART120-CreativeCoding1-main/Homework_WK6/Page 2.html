Why do I create Art?

Story Time!

My artwork is quite varied but I like to believe I specialize in color theory and the idea that involves the analytical study of color theory and feelings that are evoked through color.

My printmaking and paintings are usually very vibrant and over saturated because I like to bring life into things that are no longer useful to people through color, to make them useful once again.

Each year, my work grows and evolves into something bigger, more interesting, and better.

As an artist, there is no greater pleasure for me than finding genuine growth and improvement in my projects.
